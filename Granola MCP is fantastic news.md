For those who prioritise a centralised knowledge centre, the goal is often to ensure that every insight—whether from a quick sync or a deep-dive session—lives within a single, local-first source of truth like Obsidian.  
  
To support this workflow, here developed to bridge the gap between meeting notes and a permanent knowledge graph:  
  
🔹 Automated Obsidian Sync: An integration that flows AI summaries and rich  
metadata directly into your vault, ensuring meeting insights aren't siloed in  
a separate app.  
🔹 Claude Code Search Skill: A custom /granola-search command that allows for  
natural language querying of the entire meeting library directly from the  
terminal.  
🔹 Local Cache Extraction: A specialised/granola-transcript skill and  
Python-based extractor that retrieves full verbatim transcripts from the local  
cache for deep reference.  
  
The focus is on creating a seamless flow where AI power meets a centralised personal second brain.
